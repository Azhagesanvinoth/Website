Who We Are

WELCOME To VILLAGE INFO TECH HEALTH CARE

Village Info Tech (VIT) Health Care is a leading medical coding company based in Tamil Nadu, India, specializing in HCC, Risk Adjustment, and Outpatient (OP) coding. Since 2023, we have partnered with healthcare organizations to deliver accurate, HIPAA-compliant, and efficient coding solutions.

Our mission is to empower healthcare teams by ensuring precise documentation, reducing denials, and improving risk scores. With a focus on quality, compliance, and client success, VIT HC combines expert coders, multi-layered quality audits, and secure data practices to help our clients focus on patient care while we handle the coding workflow.

With a dedicated team of 50 coders, we have successfully completed 7 projects for 5 major healthcare companies and are expanding rapidly with new client partnerships.

Our Services

Precision. Quality. Compliance.

HCC Coding (Hierarchical Condition Categories)

Accurate HCC coding ensures proper risk adjustment and fair reimbursements. At Village Info Tech Health Care, our certified coders deliver precise ICD-10 coding, maintain HIPAA compliance, and support improved revenue cycle performance.

Outpatient Coding

Outpatient coding ensures accurate ICD-10 and CPT code assignment for clinic and hospital visits. At Village Info Tech Health Care, we deliver precise, HIPAA-compliant coding to improve claim accuracy, reduce denials, and ensure faster reimbursements.

Quality Auditing

Our QA process ensures every chart is double-checked for coding accuracy and compliance. At Village Info Tech Health Care, we maintain >98% accuracy through multi-level audits, reducing errors and ensuring clean claims for faster approvals.

Client Support & Training

We provide 24/7 client support and customized coder training programs to ensure smooth onboarding, efficient communication, and consistent quality in OP and HCC coding projects.
